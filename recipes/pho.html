The normal recipe for Pho

Ingredients

  • Beef bones
  • Tough beeef cuts
  • Flat rice noodles
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Shallot
  • Chili Grinds
  • Star Alise
  • Daikon
  • Salt,sugar and fish sauce
  • Steak

Steps

  1. Simmer and cook the broth for 4 hours with bone, add aromatics
  2. Charring onion and ginger
  3. After harsness is turned into sweetness, simmer it and extract the flavour
  4. Reheat the broth
  5. Assemble the bowls with noodles and steak
